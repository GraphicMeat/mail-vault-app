Why We Built MailVault

My friend Tomas works in logistics. Every few months he hits the same wall — his 10GB mailbox is full. Again.

His inbox is packed with CMR documents, invoices, shipping confirmations, and customer correspondence — all with heavy attachments. He can't just delete them. Regulations require him to keep these records, and he needs them to resolve disputes with customers.

So every time his mailbox fills up, he's stuck: pay for more server storage, manually export emails to folders on his desktop, or risk losing documents he's legally required to keep.

That's why we built MailVault. Sync your emails locally, free up server space, and keep everything searchable and accessible — forever. No more choosing between storage costs and compliance.

Your Emails Are at Risk

Email providers can delete, lose, or restrict access to your messages at any time. Important memories, receipts, and records disappear without warning.

Server Deletion

Emails deleted from the server are gone forever. No way to recover them.

Account Lockout

Lose access to your account and lose access to years of communication.

Cloud Dependency

No internet? No access. Your data shouldn't depend on connectivity.

Email Storage, Reimagined

MailVault combines the convenience of modern email clients with the security of local storage.

Email Management

  • Compose, reply, reply all, and forward
  • Microsoft 365 OAuth2 sign-in (no app password needed)
  • Advanced search (sender, date, attachments, folder)
  • Bulk operations (select all, batch archive/delete)

Storage & Backup

  • Maildir + .eml (RFC 5322) standard format
  • One-click archive to local storage
  • ZIP export (archived or all emails)
  • Portable import & background caching

Customization

  • Dark/light mode
  • 3-column or 2-column layout (resizable)
  • List or Chat view, view modes (All/Server/Local)
  • Per-account signatures, notifications & badges

Performance & Security

  • Virtual scrolling for large mailboxes
  • macOS Keychain credential storage
  • Native Tauri (not Electron) with auto-updater
  • Open email in new window

Store Locally. Free Your Server.

Running out of server storage? Save emails to your machine, then safely delete them from the server. Your emails stay accessible forever — offline, searchable, and fully yours.

Sync & Save

MailVault fetches your emails from the server and stores them as standard .eml files in Maildir format — one file per email, no proprietary database.

Delete from Server

Once saved locally, safely delete emails from the server to free up storage. Your mailbox stays clean, your data stays safe.

Access Anytime

Read, search, and browse all your locally saved emails — even without internet. They're yours forever.

Works as a backup too

Love your current email client? Use MailVault purely as a silent backup. It syncs in the background from your menu bar — your emails are protected even if you never open the app.
