Who Uses MailVault

Real problems MailVault solves for real people.

Small Business Email Retention

Logistics companies, accountants, and operations teams deal with invoices, shipping confirmations, and compliance documents that can't be deleted. MailVault archives everything locally so you can free up your mailbox without losing records you're legally required to keep.

  • Compliance-safe retention
  • Bulk archive by date range
  • Standard .eml files courts can read

Mailbox Cleanup

Your 10GB mailbox is full. Again. Instead of paying for more server storage or randomly deleting old emails, archive them locally with MailVault. Free up server space, keep everything searchable offline.

  • Archive + delete from server
  • Instant local search
  • No recurring storage fees

Privacy-Conscious Users

Your emails contain your most personal data. MailVault stores everything on your machine — no cloud sync, no telemetry, no tracking. Credentials stay in your OS keychain. Open source so you can verify every line of code.

  • No cloud, no tracking
  • macOS Keychain / Linux Secret Service
  • Open source

Offline Email Access

Traveling, working remotely, or just don't trust your internet? Once archived, your entire email history is available offline — fully searchable, no connection needed.

  • Full offline access
  • Works without internet
  • Local search across all accounts
