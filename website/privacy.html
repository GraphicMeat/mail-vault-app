Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Who We Are

MailVault is operated by MB Modernios Aplikacijos, a company registered in Lithuania. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Lithuanian data protection laws.

The MailVault Desktop Application

MailVault is a privacy-first desktop email client. The application:

  • Stores all data locally on your computer. Your emails, attachments, and account data never leave your machine.
  • Does not transmit data to our servers. The app connects directly to your email provider via IMAP/SMTP. We have no access to your emails.
  • Stores credentials in your operating system's keychain (macOS Keychain). We never see or store your passwords.
  • Does not include analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.
  • Is open source. You can verify all of the above by inspecting the source code.

The MailVault Website

When you visit mailvaultapp.com or interact with our website, we may collect the following:

Newsletter Subscription

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. We use this solely to send you updates about MailVault (new releases, features, tips). You can unsubscribe at any time by contacting us. Legal basis: your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)).

Contact Form

If you contact us through the website form, we collect your name, email address, and message. We use this to respond to your inquiry. Legal basis: your consent and our legitimate interest in communicating with users (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a) and (f)).

"I Want This" Votes

If you click the voting button, we store a hashed (anonymized) version of your IP address to prevent duplicate votes. We do not store your actual IP address. Legal basis: legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.
  • We do not use third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels).
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Website data (newsletter subscriptions, contact messages, votes) is stored on our web server. IP addresses are hashed using SHA-256 before storage for privacy. We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

Your Rights (GDPR)

As an EU/EEA resident, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict processing of your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form messages are retained for up to 12 months. Vote data is anonymized and retained indefinitely.

Third-Party Services

Our website loads resources from:

  • Google Fonts — to serve the Inter typeface. Google may collect your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — to serve stylesheets. No personal data is collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
